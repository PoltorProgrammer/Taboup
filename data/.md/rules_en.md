### 🎯 GAME OBJECTIVE
**Taboup** is a team-based party game inspired by the classic *Taboo*. Make your team guess the **target word** without using the **forbidden words** or breaking the rules. Move across the board and be the first to reach the finish line.

---

### 👥 TEAMS AND SETUP
*   **Two teams:** Team A and Team B
*   **Players:** Recommended even number (4, 6, 8...)
*   **Setup:** Sitting in a circle alternating teams

> [!TIP]
> **Example with 6 players:**  
> A1 - B1 - A2 - B2 - A3 - B3

*The people to your left and right are never on your team, acting as **side referees**.*

---

### 🔁 TURNS AND ROTATION
*   **Rotation:** The mobile/platform always rotates **counter-clockwise**
*   **Order:** After you, the person to your **right** plays
*   **Alternation:** Teams alternate automatically (A-B-A-B...)

---

### 🧩 ROLES IN EACH TURN
1.  **Clue Giver:** Active player describing the word
2.  **Guessers:** Teammates trying to guess
3.  **Side Referees:** Players sitting to the left and right

> [!WARNING]
> **⚠️ Important:** Side referees monitor the rules and can press "Error" if they detect violations.

---

### 🎮 PLATFORM CONTROLS
*   **✅ CORRECT:** Your team guessed the word (+1 point)
*   **➡️ SKIP:** Skip current card (maximum 3 per round)
*   **❌ ERROR:** Violation detected (ends the round immediately)
*   **⏸️ PAUSE:** Pause the timer temporarily

> [!NOTE]
> **⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts:**  
> **C** = Correct | **P** = Skip | **X** = Error | **Space** = Pause

---

### 🗣️ COMMUNICATION RULES

#### 🚫 FORBIDDEN:
*   Saying **forbidden words** or their derivatives/roots. *(e.g., If the word is "SUN", you cannot say "SUNNY")*
*   **Marking syllables** with sounds or pauses. *(e.g., If the word is "SNEAKER", don't say "It's a... mh-mh-mh")*
*   **Translating** the word into another language. *(e.g., If it's "APPLE", don't say "MANZANA")*
*   Doing **gestures or mime**. *(e.g., Don't point to someone wearing glasses if the word is "GLASSES")*
*   **Spelling** the word or part of it. *(e.g., If the word is "ONION", you cannot say "Starts with O, then N...", or "Rhymes with OPINION")*

#### ✅ ALLOWED:
*   Using **synonyms and definitions**. *(e.g., For "HOUSE", say "Home" or "Dwelling")*
*   Using **descriptive circumlocution**. *(e.g., "An object used for sitting that has four legs" for CHAIR)*
*   **Onomatopoeia**, as long as they are not forbidden. *(e.g., "Goes Meow!" for CAT)*
*   **Singing**, as long as you are careful not to mark the syllables of the target word while doing so.

---

### ⏰ TIME AND COMPLETION
*   **Standard duration:** 60 seconds per round
*   **Long round:** 120 seconds (special board spaces)
*   **Maximum skips:** 3 per round
